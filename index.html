Hello, we are Klank Records

Klank Records was founded in November 2016 by Quintin Kelly, a DJ and producer from South Africa. The record label primarily specializes in techno, tech-house, and minimal-tech genres. We offer mastering services for all approved Klank Records releases, as well as in-house designing for release artwork. Our distribution network spans over 220 digital outlets worldwide, including popular platforms like iTunes, Spotify, and Beatport. At Klank Records, we are committed to delivering high-quality music production and distribution services to our clients.

If you have any question regarding Klank Records, Please reach out! Thank you.
